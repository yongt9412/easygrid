About About

What is EasyGrid?

We are a platform that enables local producers to commercialize their surplus of renewable energy and incentivize the use of renewable among neighborhoods.

Problem

As production of renewable sources increases, so do the problems related to their use Renewables are usually generated far from cities, a fact that causes losses in the distribution process. The production of PVs within urban centers in the world is still unsatisfactory

Solution

EasyGrid is a startup that adds value to the consumption of renewables, bringing benefits to both consumers and producers. Our team works to offer urban centers the possibility of establishing local electricity production and achieving greater energy independence.
